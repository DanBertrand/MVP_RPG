Welcome to our new RPG Game

It is a Role Playing Game to be enjoyed with friend. The game can be played from 2 to 5 players.

There are 5 differents kind of characters with different capacities (attack = dmg, lifpoints = hp, mana is used to perform special moves

How to play ?

  • Open the console in your favorite browser (right click on the screen > Inspect > Console)
  • Tap "startGame();" and hit enter
  • The console ask you how many players do you want (2 to 5)
  • You have then 10 Rounds to win the fight. If nobody won by this time, the character with the most health points wins
  • For every rounds, the order of attack is random to add a bit of challenge
  • Tap "exit" and press enter in the prompt box if you'd like to quit the game
